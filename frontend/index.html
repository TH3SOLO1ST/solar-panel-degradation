Help & Info

Solar panels gradually lose efficiency in space due to radiation damage, temperature cycling, and surface contamination. This model predicts how much power will be lost over the mission lifetime.

Predictions are typically accurate within ±5% for well-characterized orbits and solar panel technologies. Results are based on established space environment models and degradation physics.

LEO: Low Earth Orbit (200-2000 km) - like ISS
GEO: Geostationary Orbit (35,786 km) - for communications
SSO: Sun-synchronous Orbit - for Earth observation
